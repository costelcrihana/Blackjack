Welcome to blackjack

This is an attempt to implement a limited version of Blackjack according to Mindit test specifications. It is very rudimental in terms of user interaction but it does the job.
At the beginning of every round the cards are generated and shuffled.
Currently the hand score is not displayed but it should be correctly calculated.
Also, in case any of the players hits a blackjack or scores over 21 points the game is automatically stopped.
There is no limit in terms of total score.
Dealer is on the left, player is on the right.

It works on Mozilla, it also works on Chrome but it has to be started with --allow-file-access-from-files option.
